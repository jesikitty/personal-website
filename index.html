A bit about me

Hello there! I'm Jesi Merrick (aka jesikitty). I'm a software developer, with a current focus on full-stack JavaScript web development. I graduated from Illinois Tech with my Bachelors and Masters in Computer Science back in 2015, and since then I've had the chance to work in a few different technologies and environments (starting with some PHP, then later NodeJS and MeteorJS).

The learning continues however! Lately I've been learning about RxJS, React, functional programming, and just overall improving my skill and knowledge when it comes to web development.

My career

Straight out of college, I started my first full-time developer position over at Wellspring Worldwide. For a short time after that, I helped a small startup get ready for its first launch, where I got to jump back into MeteorJS. It was an interesting project to work on, and I hope to see them go far. Now days, I'm over at Digital Primates where I am continuing to learn and grow as a software developer!

Outside of development work, I've also discovered an interest in teaching. The summer before my third year at Illinois Tech, I was an assistant for a summer camp that introduces middle school girls to Computer Science. This launched off a love for teaching that I did not expect! Since then, I have helped with the same camp for two other summers, as well as the Fall and Spring clubs that we run for advanced students. Whether with the STARS program at IIT (oh, hey, I helped put together their website as well!), or elsewhere, I hope to continue teaching in the future.
